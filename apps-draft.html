Personalised Closed Eco System

We are now in the era of the mobile device.

Whilst web applications remain key tools in business management, consumers are increasing looking to engage on their mobile devices in a more personalised way. If you don’t have a mobile APP you are making this more difficult for your consumers and increasing the risk that they will come across other service providers as they browse their social media and web search engines.

Your FIBODO ecosystem is designed to create a seamless integration between your services and your clients. One of the key weapons in this armoury is the tailored APP. Having an APP allows you and your customers to personalise their experience, through setting their own preferences, recognising their location and offering customised recommendations based on their engagement.

It’s a fact, typical mobile users spend more time on APPs than on web browsers (88% vs 12% - eMarketer April 2020), so why would you want to drive them elsewhere if you didn’t need to?
