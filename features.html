All the

features to succeed.

From its inception our software has been designed with philosophy of continuous consumer interaction. We understand consumers are hard won and their online experience needs to be more than simply a one way transactional journey. By eschewing the outdated shopping basket model, Fibodo empowers consumers to continuously manage their bookings, making amendments, cancellations and additions, as well as inviting their friends to join them, all within the conditions our clients determine. Our clients can then focus their resources on growing and delivering their business and not on the hidden costs and reputational damage of systems based on last century principles.

Features

for business.

Fibodo modules have been designed by successful and experienced operators to ensure they provide you with the tools that will allow you to manage your business successfully and simply. For small and single sector operators, they work as standalone modules, or for more complex operations they can be combined to deliver an integrated solution for all your needs.

Fibodo’s expertise in providing front end booking solutions, with tailored consumer journeys, revenue management and direct consumer communications. Uniquely all our systems are marketplace enabled, allowing you to create your own online marketplace, or integrate with others.

Book
Fibodo manages all of your client bookings and courses for you, so you can focus on increasing your earnings. Fibodo isn’t reliant on a shopping basket, so uniquely your clients can manage, amend and reschedule their own bookings on line, within the terms you set, so you don’t have to. This means happy customers and no administration. Fibodo is working 24 hours seven days a week, so you won’t miss new opportunities, waste time and space for clients who were unable to cancel or simply don’t show because they had no way to communicate with you. Fibodo helps you avoid emergency phone calls, disrupted bookings, or battle with a diary that simple isn’t fit for purpose and all the costs associated with fixing and auditing processes that your legacy system can’t handle.
